The Story of Bia2

Announcement:

Bia2 launched in 2003, at the dawn of the new millennium. For many decades, Bia2 has pioneered and paved ways to connect Iranians worldwide. It has served as a nexus for online entertainment and a center for online networking.

Thank you for the support and for allowing us to entertain and interact with you for all these years.

See you another time, on a new frontier.
